Course Policy

  • Academic Honesty Policy

    • The Chinese University of Hong Kong places very high importance on honesty in academic work submitted by students, and adopts a policy of zero tolerance on academic dishonesty.

    All submissions (e.g., reports) should be processed by VeriGuide first.

  • Submission Policy

    • Reports cannot be plagiarized from the Internet or generated using AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT). Quotation with proper referencing isacceptable. However, the entire article cannot consist solely of quoted content. It should include your original thoughts and writings.

    No late submission will be accepted.
